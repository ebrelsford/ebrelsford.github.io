Assignment 7

This assignment is intentionally relatively short. Please spend time working on your final project this week, and if you can work this into your final project, please do that too.

Resources

Be careful, many of the resources that exist for CartoDB use an older interface. For now start with the official documentation for Builder and email me with questions.

Make a shareable map in CARTO

Create a map in CARTO using at least two layers of data and at least one widget. This can be data you have used for an earlier assignment or that will be useful for your project. Or it could just be data you find interesting.

Share the map publicly and submit the linik to your map.

Submitting your work

Please submit the public link to your map along with a short description of what you did.
