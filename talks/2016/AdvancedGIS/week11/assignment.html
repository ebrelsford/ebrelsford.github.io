Assignment 9: Mapbox

Make a Mapbox style

  1. Create a Mapbox account if you do not have one.
  2. Starting with an existing style, customize the style. Optionally upload your own data and style it.
  3. Publish the style.
  4. Use the style somehow, such as in a Leaflet or CartoDB map.
  5. Publish your map online with Github pages.

Work on your final project

  1. When you proposed your project you included a milestone that you would be able to finish by April 19th. Work on your project and aim to meet that milestone.

Submitting your work

Submit a link to your work.

You may optionally choose to include a short paragraph response or summary of your work.
