Geoprocessing

Following steps similar to those in class 8's in-class exercise part 1, download this data and do the following:

  1. Buffer the subway entrances by 1000 feet, dissolved.
  2. Intersect the census tracts with the subway entrances.
  3. Dissolve the intersection on BoroCT2010.
  4. Use Field Calculator to add an area column to the dissolved intersections from the previous step.
  5. Join the layer from the previous step back to the original census tracts layer. You only need the resulting area field.
  6. Finally, make a choropleth of the percentage of each census tract that is within 1000 feet of a subway entrance.
