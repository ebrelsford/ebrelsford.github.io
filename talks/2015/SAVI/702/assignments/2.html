Part 1: A Small Scale Map

Pick a part of the world that you like or want to know more about. Download Natural Earth data to make a map of the region you chose. Natural Earth is a free-to-use and accurate data set for the whole world, and it is the go-to data for this type of task. You should be working at a smaller scale (that is, zoomed out), so you will likely be interested in the 10m cultural and 10m physical vector files, but feel free to use any that make sense for your map. All of the files are available on the Natural Earth downloads page.
